March 7th: The Energetic Trailblazer

A Tribute to Our Beloved Photographer

About March 7th

March 7th is a cheerful and curious member of the Astral Express in Honkai: Star Rail. Discovered frozen in space with no memory of her past, she adopted the name March 7th, the day she was rescued. Energetic and playful, she loves taking photos to create new memories. Despite her mysterious origins, she remains optimistic and ready for adventure.

In combat, March 7th wields Ice-based abilities, using her bow to freeze enemies and protect her allies. Her bright pink hair and stylish outfit match her vibrant personality, making her a fan-favorite for her positivity and charm.

Abilities

Basic Attack: Frigid Cold Arrow

Deals Ice DMG equal to 50% of March 7th's ATK to a single enemy.

Skill: The Power of Cuteness

Provides an ally with a Shield that can absorb DMG equal to 38% of March 7th's DEF +190 for 3 turns. If the ally's HP is 30% or higher, greatly increases the chance of enemies attacking that ally.

Ultimate: Glacial Cascade

Deals Ice DMG equal to 90% of March 7th's ATK to all enemies. On a hit, the enemy has a 50% base chance of being Frozen for 1 turn. While Frozen, enemies cannot take actions and will receive additional Ice DMG equal to 30% of March 7th's ATK at the beginning of each turn.

Talent: Girl Power

After a shielded ally is attacked by an enemy, March 7th immediately Counters, dealing Ice DMG equal to 50% of her ATK. This effect can be triggered 2 times each turn.

Technique: Freezing Beauty

Immediately attacks the enemy. After entering battle, there is a 100% base chance to Freeze a random enemy for 1 turn(s). While Frozen, the enemy cannot take action and will take Ice DMG equal to 50% of March 7th's ATK at the beginning of each turn.

Bonus Abilities

  • Ice Spell

    Increases Utimate's base chance to Freeze enemies by 15%.

  • Reinforce

    The duration of the Shield generated from her Skill is extended for 1 turn.

  • Purify

    Using Skill removes 1 debuff from the target.
